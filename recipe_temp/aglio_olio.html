Aglio Olio

Aglio Olio is a classic Italian pasta dish, made with spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. It's a simple yet flavorful recipe that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : pasta Italian garlic olive oil

Instructions

Step 1

Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain well and set aside.

Step 2

In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until golden brown.

Step 3

Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss until evenly coated with the garlic-infused olive oil.

Step 4

Season with salt, black pepper, and chili flakes to taste. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Ingredients

400g spaghetti

6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced

1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

1 teaspoon chili flakes

Salt and black pepper to taste

Chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese for garnish (optional)

Tools

Large pot

Skillet

Colander

Cooking spoon

Knife

Cutting board
