Roti Canai

Roti Canai is a popular Malaysian flatbread known for its flaky texture. It's usually served with dal or curry, making it a delicious and satisfying meal.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Malaysian Bread Flaky Curry

Instructions

Step 1

In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, and water. Knead until you get a smooth and elastic dough. Let it rest for 1 hour.

Step 2

Divide the dough into small balls. Roll out each ball into a thin circle on a floured surface.

Step 3

Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Cook each roti canai for about 1-2 minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy.

Step 4

Serve hot with your favorite curry or dal.

Ingredients

3 cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp salt

1 1/2 cups water

Vegetable oil for frying

Tools

Large mixing bowl

Rolling pin

Skillet or griddle

Spatula
