Nasi Lemak

Nasi Lemak is a fragrant rice dish cooked in coconut milk and pandan leaves, served with various accompaniments such as fried anchovies, peanuts, boiled eggs, cucumber slices, and spicy sambal sauce.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : malaysian rice coconut spicy

Instructions

Step 1

Wash and rinse rice, then soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain the rice and set aside.

Step 2

In a pot, combine coconut milk, pandan leaves, and salt. Bring to a boil.

Step 3

Add the drained rice to the pot and stir well. Cook over low heat until the rice is fluffy and cooked through.

Step 4

Serve the nasi lemak hot with fried anchovies, peanuts, boiled eggs, cucumber slices, and spicy sambal sauce.

Ingredients

2 cups jasmine rice

2 cups coconut milk

2 pandan leaves

1 tsp salt

Fried anchovies, peanuts, boiled eggs, cucumber slices, and spicy sambal sauce for serving

Tools

Pot

Stove

Spoon

Knife

Cutting board
