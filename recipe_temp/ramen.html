Ramen

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup dish consisting of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and topped with various ingredients such as sliced pork, seaweed, and green onions.

Prep Time

15 min.

Cook Time

30 min.

Serving

4 servings

Tags: Japanese, Noodles, Soup

Instructions

Step 1

Boil the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2

In a pot, heat the broth over medium heat. Add soy sauce, miso paste, garlic, and ginger. Stir well and let it simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 3

Cook the toppings such as sliced pork, boiled eggs, seaweed, and green onions separately.

Step 4

Divide the cooked noodles into serving bowls. Pour the hot broth over the noodles and add the cooked toppings. Serve immediately.

Ingredients

4 packs of ramen noodles

8 cups chicken or vegetable broth

2 tbsp soy sauce

2 tbsp miso paste

2 cloves garlic, minced

1-inch piece of ginger, grated

Toppings: sliced pork, boiled eggs, seaweed, green onions

Tools

Large pot

Cooking spoon

Bowls for serving

Chopsticks or fork
